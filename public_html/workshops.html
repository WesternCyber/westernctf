Western Cyber Presents Bitcoin
2 Mar. 2016
  • Location Spencer Engineering Building Room 2202
  • Type Bitcoin - Workshop

Western Cyber Security in conjunction with the Western Economics Society is proud to present, Western Cyber Presents Bitcoin. We will have Software Engineering professor Aleksander Essex, doing an intro to BITCOIN AND CRYPTOCURRENCIES and we will show participants the fundamentals of Bitcoin mining, Bitcoin transaction, and everything you need to know about using bitcoins in any upcoming projects or Hackathons. Bring a laptop and a learning attitude! Food will be provided.
