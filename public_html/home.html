Who are we?

  • About us

    The Western Cyber Security club is a student run organization based in the Computer Science Department at Western University. Our main purpose is to teach students about cyber security, hacking, its ethics, breakthroughs and applications.

  • Events

    Every month we regularly put teams together to compete at international hacking competitions. This serves as practice to the material we teach at our workshops.

  • Workshops

    We aim for 3 principles during our workshops. Application, Economics and Theory. Introduction to Hacking, Introduction to Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin), and Speaker Keynotes about Cloud Security.

What we do?

We organize monthly workshops and competitions for students wishing to learn more about the cyber security.
